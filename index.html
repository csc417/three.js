Theater 331 by csc417

A shared Movie theater for stream videos built in three.js Javascript 3D Engine as a final project for a class at the University of Southern Mississippi in 2011.

Demonstrates: streaming video & use of EasyWebSocket in webgl
Movie Theater(Tested in Chrome and Firefox)

Dependencies

three.js
EasyWebSocket

Install

none - just clone and go!
